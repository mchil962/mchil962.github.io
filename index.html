About Me

I was born and raised in Indiana and attended university in South Carolina. I earned my undergraduate degree in Criminal Justice and will be working toward my Master's of Biblical Counseling this fall.

With my degrees, I plan to start my career in social work, specifically as a case worker with foster care. For four years, I have study the topic of human trafficking and with two of those years, I studied the correlation between trafficking and individuals in the foster care system.

Outside of work and school, I spend my time reading, drawing, and hanging out with family and friends. Some nights, I can be found going to bed early but most nights, I can be found cranking out homework and papers for school sipping on a hot- well, lukewarm if we're lucky- cup of coffee and listening to music.

Thank you for coming to my website! If you have any comments or questions on any of my work or on any of my studies, feel free to reach out! Also, if you have any recommendations for books, please, let me know! My TBR list is a mile long, but I'll get to it eventually. (;
